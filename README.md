
# PowerBI Dashboard Project – Atliq Hardwares Sales Analytics

## Project Overview

This project focuses on building an interactive Sales Analytics Dashboard for Atliq Hardwares using Power BI. The objective of the project is to analyze sales performance, revenue trends, customer insights, and market-level business KPIs to support data-driven business decisions.

The dashboard provides a centralized reporting solution that helps stakeholders track overall business performance, identify top-performing markets and customers, and monitor profitability trends efficiently.

---

# Business Problem

Atliq Hardwares was facing challenges in:

* Tracking sales performance across multiple regions and customers
* Understanding revenue and profit contribution
* Managing fragmented Excel-based reporting
* Identifying declining markets and underperforming products
* Making faster business decisions using real-time insights

The goal was to transform raw business data into interactive dashboards for improved reporting visibility and strategic analysis.

# Project Workflow

## Step 1 – Requirement Understanding

* Understood business requirements and reporting objectives
* Identified key KPIs such as Revenue, Profit Margin, Sales Quantity, and Customer Contribution
* Defined dashboard goals and stakeholder expectations

---

## Step 2 – Data Collection

* Imported sales, customer, product, and market datasets
* Connected multiple data sources into Power BI
* Verified data consistency and completeness

---

## Step 3 – Data Cleaning & Transformation

* Removed duplicate and null records
* Standardized column formats
* Performed ETL transformations using Power Query
* Created relationships between tables for data modeling

---

## Step 4 – Data Modeling

* Built star-schema based data model
* Created relationships between:

  * Customers
  * Products
  * Markets
  * Transactions
  * Date Tables

---

## Step 5 – DAX Calculations

Created calculated measures for:

* Total Revenue
* Profit Margin
* Sales Quantity
* Revenue Contribution %
* Profit Percentage
* Dynamic KPI Calculations

---

## Step 6 – Dashboard Development

Designed interactive dashboards with:

* KPI Cards
* Bar Charts
* Line Charts
* Trend Analysis
* Regional Performance Insights
* Customer Contribution Analysis
* Dynamic Filters and Slicers

---

## Step 7 – Business Insights Generation

Generated insights such as:

* Top-performing markets
* High revenue customers
* Profitability trends
* Sales decline regions
* Business growth opportunities
